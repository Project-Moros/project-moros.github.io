[BLOG UPDATE] Game's and Game Count

First official update that gives something in writing for people to read! This is comming from the head developer and I have to say I'm quite excited to see where the development leads. None the less, you came here to hear about games. As for how many games will be on the site is undetermined but it should be upwards of 75. The games will include around about 30 HTML based games, 20 Adobe Flash games, and a handful of ROMs (unsure how many yet).


The majority of the ROMs are emulated using EmulatorJS, MSDOS programs are emulated using JSDOS, and the Adobe Flash games will be emulated using Ruffle. I think that's all from me so see you in the next update. (^_^ )
